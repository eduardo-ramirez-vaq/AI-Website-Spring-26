05
Key Insights

What actually surprised the researcher — pulled directly from the study findings.

🏆

Co-Pilot Think Deeper Won

The highest-rated tool wasn't ChatGPT or Gemini — it was Co-Pilot Think Deeper via EWU Copilot. Rated 5/5 for being "very informative in design" with zero limits hit.

🚧

Free Tiers Hit Walls

Perplexity required account creation to keep asking questions, and Monica also forced an account. Le Chat kept prompting for an account — though it wasn't required.

🖼️

Perplexity Stood Out Visually

Despite its low 2/5 rating (largely due to friction), Perplexity was the only model noted for "base imagery" and "very clear photo thoughts" — a unique differentiator for visual thinkers.

🔍

More Detail Isn't Always Better

ChatGPT 5.4 was described as "quite a handful" — lots of detail, but the researcher found ChatGPT 5.3 more practical for quick, clean answers. Depth vs. usability is real tension.

⚖️

Gemini 3.1 Thinking Impressed

Among Gemini models, the Thinking tier gave "much detail on the subjects in question" and was called "pretty nice" with liked approaches — suggesting reasoning models are worth the extra wait.

🎨

Design-Specific Prompts Revealed Gaps

Prompts around blind box packaging design and type hierarchy separated the tools. Some excelled at structure (Le Chat), others at direction (ChatGPT), and others at deep detailing (Co-Pilot Think Deeper).
